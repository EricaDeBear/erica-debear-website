# Fair Housing Notice

**Equal Housing Opportunity — The Rise Group is an equal opportunity provider.**
**Applicable Law:** Federal Fair Housing Act · California FEHA · Unruh Civil Rights Act · LA Municipal Ordinances

> **Equal Housing Opportunity.** The Rise Group is committed to providing equal housing opportunities to all persons. We fully comply with the federal Fair Housing Act, the California Fair Employment and Housing Act (FEHA), the Unruh Civil Rights Act, and all applicable Los Angeles municipal fair housing ordinances. We do not discriminate in the provision of real estate services on the basis of any protected characteristic.

---

## 1. Federal Fair Housing Act

The Federal Fair Housing Act (42 U.S.C. §§ 3601–3631) prohibits discrimination in the sale, rental, and financing of housing based on the following protected classes:

- Race
- Color
- National Origin
- Religion
- Sex
- Familial Status
- Disability

---

## 2. California Fair Employment and Housing Act (FEHA) and Unruh Civil Rights Act

California law provides broader protections than federal law. Under the FEHA (Gov. Code §§ 12900 et seq.) and the Unruh Civil Rights Act (Civ. Code § 51), The Rise Group does not discriminate based on any of the following protected characteristics:

- Race
- Color
- National Origin
- Religion
- Sex / Gender
- Gender Identity
- Gender Expression
- Sexual Orientation
- Marital Status
- Familial Status
- Disability (Physical or Mental)
- Medical Condition
- Genetic Information
- Age (40 and over)
- Ancestry
- Source of Income
- Immigration / Citizenship Status
- Primary Language
- Veteran / Military Status
- Any Other Arbitrary Basis

> California's fair housing laws cover virtually all housing, with very limited exceptions. Unlike federal law, California does not exempt most owner-occupied single-family housing from fair housing requirements.

---

## 3. Los Angeles Municipal Fair Housing

The City of Los Angeles and Los Angeles County provide additional fair housing protections administered by the **Los Angeles Housing Department (LAHD)**. The Rise Group complies with all applicable city and county ordinances, including protections based on:

- **Source of income** (including housing vouchers / Section 8)
- **Criminal history** (in accordance with applicable "fair chance housing" ordinances)
- Any other locally protected characteristics

**To report housing discrimination in the City of Los Angeles, contact:**

- **LA Housing Department (LAHD):** [housing.lacity.gov/residents/fair-housing](https://housing.lacity.gov/residents/fair-housing)
- **California Civil Rights Department (CRD):** [calcivilrights.ca.gov/housing](https://calcivilrights.ca.gov/housing) | 1-800-884-1684
- **HUD Fair Housing:** 1-800-669-9777 | [hud.gov/fairhousing](https://www.hud.gov/fairhousing)

---

## 4. Our Commitment

All agents, employees, and contractors of The Rise Group are required to comply with fair housing laws and our internal Equal Housing Opportunity policy. We provide fair housing training to all team members and take all complaints of discriminatory conduct seriously.

We do **not**:

- Steer clients toward or away from neighborhoods based on any protected characteristic ("steering");
- Apply different terms, conditions, or standards in the representation of buyers or sellers based on protected characteristics;
- Make, print, or publish any advertisement that indicates a preference, limitation, or discrimination based on any protected class;
- Represent that a property is unavailable when it is available;
- Deny any person access to our services on the basis of any protected characteristic.

---

## 5. Reasonable Accommodations

We will provide reasonable accommodations and modifications to our services for persons with disabilities as required by the Fair Housing Act and California law. If you need an accommodation or modification to access our real estate services, please contact us and we will work with you to address your needs.

---

## 6. Filing a Complaint

If you believe you have experienced discrimination in violation of fair housing laws in connection with our services, you may:

1. **Contact us directly** at la@therise.group;
2. **File a complaint with the California Civil Rights Department (CRD)** within one (1) year of the alleged discriminatory act at [calcivilrights.ca.gov](https://calcivilrights.ca.gov) or 1-800-884-1684;
3. **File a complaint with HUD** within one (1) year of the alleged act at [hud.gov/fairhousing](https://www.hud.gov/fairhousing) or 1-800-669-9777; or
4. **File a civil lawsuit** in state or federal court.
